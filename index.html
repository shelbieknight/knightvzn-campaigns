Terms & Working Agreement

How we work together

This is a month-to-month retainer with no long-term commitment required. Either of us can end the engagement with 30 days' written notice.

Billing

Invoices go out on the 1st of each month and are due within 7 days. To kick things off, a $500 onboarding deposit is collected upfront and applied in full to your first invoice.

Your campaign brief

To hit your turnaround window, we need your completed campaign brief by the 5th of each month. The sooner it's in, the sooner we can get to work.

Video & reels

We don't produce original video content, but if you have footage ready to post, we'll handle scheduling at no extra charge. Clips that need editing are billed at $150 each.

Revisions

Each tier includes a set number of revision rounds (check your package above). Need more? Additional rounds are billed at $75/hr and we'll always let you know before we start.

Content approval

You'll have 3 business days to review and approve your monthly content calendar. If we don't hear back, we'll take that as a green light and move forward on schedule.

Platforms

We'll post on the 2 platforms you choose at onboarding. Want to add a third? That's +$400/month. Platform switches require 30 days' notice so we can plan accordingly.

Paid advertising

Ad spend isn't included in any tier; that budget goes directly to the platform and stays in your control. We can create static ad creatives as an add-on, but we don't manage ad accounts or campaigns.
